About Saurav Pandey

Saurav Pandey is the founder of Vision_S, a futuristic creative brand exploring the intersection of AI, design, and innovation. Originally from Birgunj, Nepal, he completed his schooling at Kadambari Academy, Shreepur, where he graduated with a perfect GPA of 4.0 in the S.E.E. examination. Currently, he is pursuing his +2 studies in the Science stream with Biology at St. Xavier’s College, Maitighar, Kathmandu. Driven by curiosity and creativity, Saurav combines his love for artificial intelligence, visual design, and technology to build projects that bridge imagination and reality. Through Vision_S, he experiments with futuristic logo designs, motion effects, AI-powered tools, and interactive web experiences that express individuality and modern aesthetics. Saurav’s vision is to create a world where technology amplifies human creativity, helping people design, communicate, and build more intuitively. He believes in open learning, innovation, and inspiring young minds to see technology not just as a tool, but as a canvas for limitless creation.
