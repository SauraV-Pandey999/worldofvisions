VISION_S FAQ

1. What is VISION_S?
VISION_S is a company founded by Saurav Pandey in 2023. It started with limited resources but a grand vision: to become an undisputed leader brand providing services across every sector. Currently, it helps students in Nepal with study resources and is building a dynamic team of young visionary minds.
2. How can I join the VISION_S team?
To join the VISION_S team, you must understand the company's vision, demonstrate a hardworking and dedicated spirit, abide by rules, and submit all required documents and information during the application. Here is the link for the membership form
3. What roles are available at VISION_S?
Available roles include Developer, Designer, Marketing Specialist, Intern, Freelancer, Content Creator, Community Manager, Research & Development.
4. Do you offer internships or freelance opportunities?
Yes! VISION_S provides internships and freelance opportunities. These are unpaid but are designed to help you grow, learn, and develop skills while contributing to the company's vision.
5. How does VISION_S handle user privacy and data?
VISION_S is committed to protecting your privacy. Any data you share is securely stored, used only for official purposes, and never shared with third parties without consent. See our Privacy Policy for details.
