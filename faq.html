About Vision-S
01What is VISION-S?
About
+
VISION-S is a company founded by Saurav Pandey in 2023. It started with limited resources but a grand vision: to become an undisputed leader brand providing services across every sector. Currently, it helps students in Nepal with study resources and is building a dynamic team of young visionary minds.
02Is VISION-S a physically registered company?
About
+
VISION-S is currently a digitally-based company — operating fully online, serving students and building teams across borders without a physical office. However, this is only the beginning. Vision-S is actively working towards physical operations in the near future, expanding its reach beyond the digital world into real spaces where impact can be felt directly.
03What is Saurav's long-term goal for VISION-S?
About
+
The long-term goal is nothing short of extraordinary — to evolve Vision-S into a global, all-sector powerhouse and the undisputed #1 brand in every field it enters. Not for vanity, but because scale means reach, and reach means more students, more communities, and more lives transformed forever. Saurav wants the entire world to see with great vision, influenced and inspired by him.
04Where does VISION-S currently operate?
About
+
VISION-S currently operates in Nepal and Switzerland — with Nepal being the founding ground where it all began, and Switzerland marking the company's first step toward international expansion. As a digitally-based company, Vision-S is accessible globally, and more countries will follow as the team and mission grow.
05What makes VISION-S different?
About
+
VISION-S stands out because of its young leaders with a great vision. The company values innovation, teamwork, and creating opportunities for growth — making it unique in both approach and execution. It was built by a student, for students, with global ambition. There is no age barrier, no corporate gatekeeping — just passion, creativity, and relentless drive.

Team

07 Is there an age requirement to join?NEW
Team
+
There is no age restriction at Vision-S. We wholeheartedly encourage young minds with creativity and the potential to add real value. Team
+
After you submit your form, our team reviews your application within 24 hours. If everything checks out and you meet our standards, you will receive your official Vision-S Membership Card within 24 hours of approval. It's fast, it's real, and it's your first step into the Vision-S family.

Team
